PALAKKAD

Palakkad is the gateway to Kerala due to the presence of the Palakkad Gap, in the Western Ghats. The district is nicknamed "the granary of Kerala". The district is blessed with many small and medium rivers, which are tributaries of the Bharathapuzha River.

Silent Valley National Park

Silent Valley national park has some rare species of flora and fauna. It is located in the rich biodiversity of Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve. This area was explored in 1847 by the botanist Robert Wight.
