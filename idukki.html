IDUKKI

Idukki is a land-locked district that is famous as one of the most nature rich areas in Kerala. Image result for idukki tourist spots Idukki district is famous for its rich flora and fauna. it is the second-largest district in the region in terms of area, it has the lowest population density among the districts of Kerala
Munnar

Munnar is a popular hill station of Kerala perched at an altitude of 1600 metres in the Western Ghats. Famous for its tea estates, various shades of green, blankets of mist forming natural-view points, Munnar is aptly known as the 'Kashmir of South India'.

Idukki Arch Dam

Idukki Arch Dam, an engineering marvel. It is an arch dam with a double curvature and is built on River Periyar. e dam is constructed in a gorge between two hills, Kuravathi and Kuravan. It is at the height of about 550 feet

Periyar National Park

The Periyar National Park in Thekkady, Kerala, is one of the most bio-diverse regions in the world and the best-protected reserve area.amous for its gorgeousness, greenery and stillness, the park is the dwelling place of abundant significant species, including the royal tigers and majestic elephants apart from other reptiles, fishes and birds.
